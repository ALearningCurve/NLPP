Welcome!

How Do I Start?

All you need to join in on the fun is a free account. To make an account, simply go to the Sign Up Page, or if you already made an account, go to the Login Page. With that out of the way, you can join your first group

Whats a group?

Groups are where you and others can join and view the same filtered and posted content!

Joining a Group

To join a group simply obtain a join code from someone already in the group, and then you can go to the Find a Group Page. Once you joined the group that was searched, it will be added to My Groups Page. In the My Groups Page you can see all the groups you have joined or made.

Whats in a Group?

Once you have joined a group, you now have acess to all of the posts within that group! This means that you can begin interacting with all of the posts that the group owner has put into the group. Want to leave the group? Where the join button used to be it will now say leave, you just have to click it once and you'll be out of there.

I am the creator

As the creator you can see all of the member of your group and then kick out unwanted members. To kick a member click on the on the members button near the top of the page and scroll through the list of members to then kick that member.

Whats a Post?

Posts are where you and other can interact with readings to gain a greator understanding of the content.

Finding a Post

to find a post you must go the group that the post is in, have joined the group, and then scroll through the posts until you find the correct one. Posts are ordered by date created, so new posts are at the top and older posts are near the bottom.

Whats in a Post

Once you click on the post you will see the title of the post with the description and some other data like due-date under that. Underneath the description, you will see the body of the post. This is where you read the information and then are able to double click words.

Double Clicking?

When you double click a word, a modal (a drop down) will appear telling you the synonym of the word that you clicked on. If you are still unsure of what the word means, you can get a direct translation by clicking the more info button. When you double click a word it will be added to the list of single clicked words(synonyms) and when you click more info it will be added to the list of double clicked words(translations) to view these lists go to the post, and click the interaction info button.

Interaction Information

The words you double click or request more info about will be stored and to view these words click on the interaction info button on the post details page. When you click the button a modal (a drop down) will appear with your name. Simply click your name to reveal the categories (single click (synonym) and double click (translate)). Clicking one of these categories will reveal a table with the words that you have clicked. You must of been in the group when the post was assigned to view this information. If you joined the group after the post was made, your click data will not be tracked

I am the creator

As the creator you can:

Make a Post

To make a post you must be the creator of the group. If you are the creator of the group, click on the group from My Groups and then click on the Add Post button which will take you to a form you must complete. Fill in the feilds appropiatly, but if you want to stlye the body (the main content) then use the provided TinyMCE styling at the top of the textbox. Also, for due dates follow the strict format of 'year-month-day hr:min:sec'. From lang[uage] is the language the source material is in and To lang[uage] is the language for the text to be translated into.

Uploads

Posts allow you to extract text from images (".bmp", ".pnm", ".png", ".jfif", ".jpeg", ".jpg"), word docs (".doc", ".docx"), and pdf (".pdf"). To use this feature scroll to the bottom of the form and under the Post! button click the button "text upload and extraction". Both pdfs and images that are uploaded use OCR word recognition and word docs that are uploaded will have the text and stlying directly extracted. This means that the image and pdf text extractions are subject to error, so if your pdf is is 100% text use a pdf text extractor instead.

See Interaction Information

As the creator you can view the post member interaction information for all of the members who were in the group at the time the post was assigned. see double clicking and interaction information for more information of what this data means.

Set and See Completion

As the creator you can see wether or not a user had completed an assignment and upon completion you can see the date and if it was done on time. If it was done on time, the font will show the date in green, but if it was late or incomplete then the font will red. Clicks required for completion can be set when creating or updating the post. the minimun required clicks is 0 and the maximum required clicks is 100.
